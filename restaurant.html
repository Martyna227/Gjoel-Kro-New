The restaurant

Unforgettable experience

Gjoel Kro is a special place where you can try Danish traditional food. We offer you to experience HYGGE. In cozy place surrounded by positive people. Our place is perfect for sailors, hikers but also for families and for planning social events.

History

Gjol Kro was built in 1902. In the begging was well known by sailors visiting Limfjord harbour. In 1925 Danish author Hans Kirk stayed in Gjol Inn and researched for his novel "Fiskene" also. This novel was filmed in 1975 and it was using Inn as the main scene.
